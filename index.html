Who We Are

A Construction Company
Built on Family Values

At JGC Construction, we believe that building a home or renovating a space is more than just a job. It's a trust that our customers place in us. We take that seriously.

Founded right here in our community, JGC Construction was born from a simple idea: treat every customer the way we'd want to be treated. That means honest communication, fair pricing, showing up on time, and delivering work we're genuinely proud of.

Whether it's a kitchen remodel, a new build, or a commercial project, we bring the same dedication and attention to detail. Your satisfaction isn't just our goal. It's our reputation.
